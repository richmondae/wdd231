Overview

Purpose

This website is designed to promote and facilitate the rental of electric bikes. It will provide users with detailed pricing information, an intuitive reservation system, and clear contact options. Whether a commuter, tourist, or eco-conscious traveler, users will find everything they need to rent an e-bike easily and efficiently.

Audience

Eco-conscious commuters, tourists, students, and city explorers looking for affordable and sustainable transportation options.
