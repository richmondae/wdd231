Attractions

Fun fest 2025

Address: Zilker Park

The calabar fun Festival brings together artists, industry professionals, and music lovers from around the world to make connections, elevate their careers, and celebrate the magic of live music from legendary performers to the most exciting new talent.

Calabar Museum

Address: No 4 Main Avenue

The Calabar Museum offers 3 full floors of exhibits featuring everything about Calabar also includes a 3D Imax Experience.

Calabar Nature & Science Center

Address: CICC Drive.

The Calabar Nature & Science Center offers many programs for preschoolers, kids, families, and adults with topics ranging from butterflies to caving and much more.

Marina Resort

Address: Finger park

Marina resort race track, concerts, plus COTA Karting. Go carts with speeds up to 45mph. Premier carting for all skill levels.

Calabar Gardens

Address: Ekorinm Springs Rd.

Tucked away on 19 acres of land by the road side, these gardens are a lush sanctuary of koi ponds, live oaks, and prickly cactuses of all shapes and sizes. They are composed of multiple themed gardens

Kwa Falls

Address: Akampa

While the river itself is reason enough to visit, scenic trails wind through forests, atop ridges, and along the riverside. Walk the sandy shores of the Kwa River and climb on the river’s rock formations.

Adiabong Lady Lake

Address: Street Adiaobong.

It wasn't until they finished the boardwalk on Lady Lake that you could circle the entire trail without a few treks on the road. Now there's a full 10.2 mile loop along the water in downtown Calabar.
